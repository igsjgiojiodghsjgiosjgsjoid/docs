## What is Aura?

Aura is like **Cursor for images and videos** - it provides access to the best and most hyper-realistic image & video generation APIs through a simple, unified platform. Instead of dealing with multiple API providers, complex setups, or recurring monthly subscriptions, Aura offers a credit-based system that lets you pay only for what you create.
